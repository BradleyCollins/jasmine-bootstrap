Clinical Supervision

Clinical Supervision / Clinical Consultation


I provide clinical supervision for those who need it to obtain their hours for the Board of Behavioral Sciences or for those who would like some extra supervision and their current work place/private practice does not provide it. I have special training in reflective supervision that allows me to provide the emotional support that is needed in this field.

A variety of topics are covered during supervision that consist of but not limited too: counter-transference issues, transference, diagnosis, assessment, intervention suggestions etc. My goal is to provide clinicians with a space to get support, guidance, and meet them where they are at in their professional development process.

My Supervision rate is $100 per individual session Or $50.00 per participant for group supervision.
