Trauma Informed

Most people have been impacted by a traumatic experience at some point in their life


The trauma can vary from: having an abusive (physical, sexual, emotional) childhood, car accident, being a victim of a crime, death, losing a child, divorce etc., the list can go on and on. If the traumatic experience is causing issues for you, such as: flashbacks, avoidance of triggers, agitation, or you just need a place to process the experience, then my therapeutic services are what you need.

I have over 10 years of experience with treating patients who have suffered a variety of different types of trauma. I have attended numerous trainings and conferences to better understand the impact that trauma can have on a person. I have received certification in over 4 treatment models that have been proven in research studies to decrease the impact of the trauma for clients and help them recover and heal.

I have been certified in the following trauma informed evidence based practices: Seeking Safety, Trauma Focused-Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Child-Parent Psychotherapy, and Caring for our Families. All of these treatment models help to incorporate the impact of the trauma into the therapeutic process to help provide clients the tools they need to manage their trauma in the present and help them become survivors rather than victims.

If you are ready to feel a relief from your trauma, please give me a call and I will help you through your journey.
