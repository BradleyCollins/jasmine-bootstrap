Services

I work with a wide range of emotional and behavioral issues providing services that span from therapy for depression and grief counseling to parenting support, stress management and beyond. In a safe, comfortable, and supportive atmosphere, I offer a highly personalized approach tailored to each of my clients individual needs to help attain the personal growth they’re striving for.

Services Offered

EMDR Therapy Couples therapy Therapy for Depression and/or Anxiety Miscarriage, stillborn, and child bereavement
Family Counseling Phase of life changes Parenting Skills/Healthy Co-parenting Grief Counseling
Trauma Therapy Therapy for Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) Work and Career issues Stress Management

Rates

Sliding scale from $125 (individual therapy) to $175 (couples or family therapy) per 50-minute session. A special "meet and greet" introductory 30 min session at the rate of $75 to see if you feel comfortable with my services.

Reduced Fee

Reduced fee services are available on a limited basis.

Payment

Cash, check (made out to Jasmine Adams), or major credit cards.

Accepted Insurance

I accept MHN/HealthNet and Cigna as well as provide a superbill for insurances that have out of network benefits.

Cancellation Policy

If you do not show up for your scheduled therapy appointment, and you have not notified me at least 24 hours in advance, you will be required to pay the session rate. Emergency situations will be evaluated on a individual basis.
